About Us

Kiran Industries is a family owned business with over 30 years experience. The company has more than a decade of CNC Machining experience. We have manufactured some of the most Critical Components in the engineering sector. Our high-tech CNC workshop offers custom-made engineering parts as per customer's drawings and specifications. Our workshop is equipped with modern facilities, machinery and skilled manpower to meet the stringent demands of the engineering industry.

With years of experience and excellence we have manufacture CNC Power chucks to suit our Indian market and Abroad.

KIRAN INDUSTRIES has developed POSITIVE LOK SYSTEMs to provide quality power chucks at competitive prices to the companies in India and all over the United States of America, South America and Canada. Our goal is to provide quality products at competitive price with faster delivery. We become a source that you can depend on.

Our warehouse is located in Houston, Texas which puts us in the center of the Oil & Gas Industry. We have a large Inventory of soft jaws and power chucks that can be used to meet our customers needs.
