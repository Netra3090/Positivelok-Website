Our Story

Built on Precision,
Driven by Innovation

Kiran Industries is a family-owned business with over 30 years of experience in precision engineering. With more than a decade of dedicated CNC machining expertise, we have manufactured some of the most critical components in the engineering sector.

Our high-tech CNC workshop offers custom-made engineering parts as per customer's drawings and specifications. We are equipped with modern facilities, advanced machinery, and skilled manpower to meet the stringent demands of the engineering industry.

Under the POSITIVE LOK SYSTEMS brand, we provide quality power chucks at competitive prices to companies across India, the United States of America, South America, and Canada.
